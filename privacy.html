We believe in being transparent about how we operate. Here's everything you need to know — in plain language.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The short version: We collect only what we need. We never sell your data. We never run ads. Your privacy is not a feature — it's the foundation.

Who we are

A Little Help?! Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in Arlington, TX (EIN: 35-2983400). We operate a free community app that connects neighbors through small acts of kindness.

What we collect

To create an account, we collect your email address, first name, and zip code. Your first name and zip code are shown publicly in the app. Your email address is never displayed publicly and is used only for account verification and communication from us.

We do not collect your last name, phone number, home address, payment information, or any government-issued identification.

How we use your information

We use your information to operate the app, verify your account, show you posts near your location, and communicate with you about your account or the app. We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

What we never do

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. We will never run ads in the app or on this website. We will never use your data to build advertising profiles.

Messages

Messages sent between users are private. However, messages may be reviewed by our team in response to a safety report or violation of our terms. This is disclosed to all users at signup.

Data retention

You may delete your account at any time from within the app. Upon deletion, your posts and messages are removed from the public feed. If you have questions about data removal, contact us at megan@alittlehelpapp.org.

Children

A Little Help?! is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at megan@alittlehelpapp.org.
