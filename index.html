The Problem

We're lonelier than ever.
But we don't have to be.

"Most of us are one hard week away from needing help we're too afraid to ask for."

The U.S. Surgeon General declared loneliness a public health crisis in 2023. Neighbors live side by side without knowing each other. The gap between need and generosity isn't a lack of caring — it's a lack of connection.

💬

Asking is the hardest part

Shame, pride, and fear of burdening others keep people suffering in silence — while willing neighbors sit just blocks away.

🏘️

Connection is broken

People move into neighborhoods and never meet their neighbors. Community has been replaced by screens and strangers.

💙

Giving heals too

Research shows that helping others reduces stress, increases purpose, and strengthens mental health. Everyone benefits.

🌱

Small acts matter

A ride to a doctor's appointment. A meal. A listening ear. Small acts of help between neighbors are one of the most powerful forces for community healing.

Simple by Design

Five steps to show up for each other

A Little Help?! is built to get out of the way. No ratings, no transactions, no pressure. Just neighbors connecting.

1

Sign up

Email only. First name and zip code shown publicly. Nothing else.

2

Set location

Enter your zip. Browse posts within 1, 5, 10, or 25 miles.

3

Post or browse

Ask for help or offer it across 11 categories.

4

Connect privately

Message neighbors directly. Contact info never shared.

5

Mark it done

One act of kindness added to the community counter.
