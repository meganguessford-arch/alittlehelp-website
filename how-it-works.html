How It Works

Simple by design.
Powerful by intention.

No ratings. No transactions. No pressure. Just neighbors connecting through small acts of kindness.

Five Steps

From signup to showing up

1

Create an account

Email signup with confirmation required. Only your first name and zip code are ever shown publicly. Nothing else. Ever.

2

Set your location

Enter your zip code. The app automatically detects your city and shows you posts within 1, 5, 10, or 25 miles — your choice.

3

Post a need or an offer

Post a request for help or an offer to give it across 11 categories. Be as specific or as simple as you want. No judgment here.

4

Connect privately

Message neighbors directly inside the app. Contact info is never shared automatically. You stay in control of your privacy.

5

Mark it done 🌱

When help is given and received, mark your post fulfilled. One more act of kindness added to the community Kindness Counter.

Safety First

Built with safety at every layer.

A Little Help?! is designed for real humans — which means trust and safety aren't an afterthought. They're built into every step.

🔞

Age gate — 18+

Users must confirm they're 18 or older before proceeding. No exceptions.

✅

Email confirmation

Every account requires email confirmation before activation.

🛡️

Safety disclaimer

Four-point safety agreement required at signup — meet in public, trust your instincts, not an emergency service, no licensed professionals.

🚫

Report & block

Report any user with reason and detail. Block users — their posts disappear from your feed permanently.

🔒

Privacy-first

Only first name and zip code are public. Contact info is never shared automatically. Data is never sold.

👁️

Message review policy

Messages may be reviewed following a safety report. This is disclosed to all users upfront.
